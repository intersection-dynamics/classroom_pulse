# Privacy Policy for Classroom Pulse

**Last updated: December 31, 2024**

## Overview

Classroom Pulse is a student focus tracking tool designed for teachers. This app is built with privacy as a core principle.

## Data Collection

**Classroom Pulse does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All data you enter into the app (class rosters, student names, tracking sessions) is stored locally on your device only. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

## Data Storage

All app data is stored in your device's local application storage:
- Class configurations (JSON files)
- Session records (JSON files)
- Exported CSV files

This data remains on your device unless you manually export and share it.

## Internet Access

Classroom Pulse does not require internet access to function. The app works entirely offline.

## Third-Party Services

Classroom Pulse does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

While Classroom Pulse is designed for teachers to track classroom engagement, student names entered into the app are stored locally on the teacher's device only and are never transmitted externally. The app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) by not collecting any data from children.

## Data Deletion

To delete all app data:
- Android: Go to Settings > Apps > Classroom Pulse > Storage > Clear Data
- Or simply uninstall the app

## Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in app updates with an updated "Last updated" date.
